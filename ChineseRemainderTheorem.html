Chinese Remainder Theorem

Let m1, m2,...,mn be pairwise relatively prime integers. The system of congruences

x ≡ a1 (mod m1)
x ≡ a2 (mod m2)
          .
          .
          .
x ≡ an (mod mn)

has a unique solution modulo the product of all of the moduli: (m1)(m2)...(mn)
