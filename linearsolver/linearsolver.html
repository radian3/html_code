Let x1, ... , x10 be variables. Also, for 1 ≤ r ≤ 10 and r ∈ Ζ, let ar, br, cr, dr, er, fr, gr, hr, ir, jr, and kr be constants.

Solutions

This calculator will compute the point of intersection for up to 10 equations and 10 unknowns (if one exists). For example,
one equation one unknown would be: a1x1 = b1 or two equations two unknowns would be: a1x1 + b1x2 = c1 and a2x1 + b2x2 = c2
where x1,...,x10 are calculated to satisfy the intersection of all equations.
